Farmbot is a opensource agricultural robot project by 
[farmbot.io](https://farmbot.io/).
First working copy of the project was built for the exhibition in Tsuruoka Art Forum, during the exhibition
[Agricultural Revolution 3.0](http://agri-revolution3.com/) organized by [Object of null,Inc.](http://o-n-inc.com/index.html).
